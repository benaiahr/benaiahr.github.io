Reflections on 2019

31.12.2019

Happy New Year! It's been a long time since I wrote something again, and I have nothing to do in the office (procrastinating) so I'm using this time to write about 2019.
Just today, I was reading a thread about listing out the goods and the bads of 2019. I thought it'll be cool to do it here.
So here's a short reflection list and some goals for next year.

Good:
  • Accepted into and started uni, decent grades for first sem
  • Moved onto adults group in church
  • First full year of working full-time and doing fairly well at work
  • Got decent bonus and savings
  • Mentally positive and physically healthy
  • Hit 100kg DL
  • Improved family relationships
  • New side hustles and improved drive to do more
  • Reconnected with old friends
Bad:
  • Spiritually not as good as it used to, struggle with bad habits
  • Been super busy and insufficient time to do everything I want to do
  • Grandma is still in care home, didn't visit as often, didn't bring her to church
  • Uncle is in jail for drug use (!!!)
Things to continue doing in 2020
  • Being generous
  • Positivity
  • Social life and rekindling past friendships
New goals for 2020:
  • Do 3 sticker runs!
  • 160kg DL
  • Maintain grades, 2nd upper!
  • New job
  • Write at least 1 post a month
  • Plan my schedule better with my planner [No double bookings, habit to follow things through]

There's probably more things to add but that's about all I care to do right now! Something I learned about goals awhile back is that they have to be achievable, quantifiable and time-specific. I hope this will help future-me in reflection and remind me to do all these things. See you next month, let's hope I don't forget this instantly after I publish!
